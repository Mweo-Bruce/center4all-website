OUR SERVICES AND FEATURES

Educating the Community on:

Garbage Collection

We involve community leaders,schools and religious organizations in planning and implementation. We also develop educational materials such as posters, brochures and videos that highlight the importance of garbage collection and the positive impact it has on our environment.

HIV/AIDs

We provide age-appropriate sex education in schools and community settings. Emphasize the importance of safe sex practices, condom use and abstinence. We address the issue of stigmatisation associated with HIV/AIDs by promoting open dialogue and education.

Early Pregnancies

We teach young people life skills such as problem-solving, decision-making and goal-setting. Helping them develop the skills they need to make informed life choices about their sexual health and future. We also help create economic opportunities for young people, especially girls, to reduce their vulnerability to early pregnancies.

Waste Management

We promote the concept of waste segregation at the source. Provide clear guidelines on how to separate recyclable materials, organic waste and hazardous waste. Encourage the use of compost bins for organic waste and recycling bins for recyclable materials.

SUPPORT US
